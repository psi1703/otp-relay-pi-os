How to use PAM

Steps

  1. Connect to the RTA VPN.
  2. Open the PAM URL.
  3. Enter your credentials:
  4. rtadom\IITS_*USERNAME*
  5. the 6-digit TOTP from Oracle Authenticator
  6. Search for your RTA account.
  7. Open the Connect dropdown and choose PSM-RDP.
  8. Enter a reason and select the required server IP address.
